📄 User Guideline

How to use the EduEnglish Kids multimedia learning system.

1️⃣

Open the Website

Start from the homepage and choose a learning module from the navigation menu.

2️⃣

Learn Vocabulary

Open the vocabulary page, read the word meaning, and press Listen to hear pronunciation.

3️⃣

Practice Listening

Open the listening page, play the audio, and select the correct answer.

4️⃣

Take the Quiz

Answer all questions and check your final score after completing the quiz.

5️⃣

View Multimedia Concepts

Open the multimedia page to understand media elements, compression, data security, and signal processing.
